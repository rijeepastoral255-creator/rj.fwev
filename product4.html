LOW CUT/NO-SHOW SOCKS

Nike low-cut socks are lightweight, breathable athletic socks designed to sit just below the ankle for a clean, minimal look. They typically feature soft, moisture-wicking fabric and a snug fit that provides comfort and support during everyday wear or sports activities.

MID CUT/QUARTER SOCKS

Nike mid-cut socks are designed to rise above the ankle to the mid-calf, offering a balance of coverage and breathability. They provide a snug, supportive fit with soft, moisture-wicking fabric, making them ideal for sports, workouts, or everyday wear while adding a bit more protection and style than low-cut socks.

HIGH/CREW SOCKS

Nike high-cut socks extend up toward the calf or just below the knee, offering maximum coverage and support. They’re made with soft, moisture-wicking fabric for all-day comfort and a secure fit, making them ideal for sports, training, or a bold, streetwear-inspired look.
